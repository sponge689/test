Tips for picking the best deposit option for your containers!

We offer three types of container deposit options!

1: Self Serve - This is the most commonly used option, its simple you just run your containers through the machine and print out a receipt when your done! Scan it at the CRT Machine and choose for the money to either go into your scheme id or cash!

2: Bag Drop - This option is my personal favorite, you simply drop off your bags and give the concierge your scheme id and name and they will count and deposit your funds to your account within three business days.

3: Pickup Service - This option is very similar to bag drop, however we just come to your place to pickup your containers! To be eligible you require a scheme id and at least 500 containers.
